Data visualisation in Tableau

Here are a few visualizations I made with Tableau. I really enjoy this tool, especially because you can create really sleek looking dashboards, it's miles above what Excel or Sheets can do. I will continuously add to this page as I create more visualizations. Please note that these dashboards look their best from a computer screen.

Sleep in the USA

I made this visualization with some data on self-reported sleep habits for various age groups in the USA, split by gender. The data is available here.

Bus safety in London

Interactive visualization showing breakdowns of various types of incidents in the boroughs of London. This was part of a lesson in Tableau from Coursera, but I made this from scratch and added my own flair. Data was downloaded here.
