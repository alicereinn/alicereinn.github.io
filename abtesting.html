A/B Testing Project

I realise it's kind of hard to show off by means of projects that you know how an A/B test is done, because you do need the business context and the data collection over time, and just downloading a dataset won't cut it. After all, much of the work is actually selecting and validating your relevant metrics, as well as deciding how much data you will need to reach a conclusion, all things a cookie cutter dataset does not allow you to do. However, I got the chance to actually simulate and perform an A/B test through Udacity's introductory course, which is pretty great. They set up the whole thing so that you can get some amount of freedom in your testing choices, and provided some realistic data inspired by an experiment that they actually did in the past. So I tackled the project, and here are my results! The interactive report (in Streamlit) is embedded below, or available here, and the code can be found here.
